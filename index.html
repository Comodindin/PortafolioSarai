About Me

I am an Interaction Designer with a strong focus on bridging technical development, data visualization, and user-centered experiences. I specialize in turning complex data and social research into impactful, interactive web interfaces and cartographic narratives.

My toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ArcGIS Experience Builder, alongside programming skills in Unity and Unreal Engine, and the strategic integration of AI tools. This allows me to bridge frontend development and interactive systems to build solutions that are as functional as they are visually compelling.
